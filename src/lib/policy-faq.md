# Policy FAQ

## Shipping

We offer the following shipping options:

- **Standard Shipping (5–7 business days):** Free on orders over $50; $4.99 for orders under $50.
- **Express Shipping (2–3 business days):** $9.99 flat rate.
- **Overnight Shipping:** $19.99 flat rate, order by 12 pm local time.

Orders are processed Monday–Friday (excluding public holidays). You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. We currently ship to the US, Canada, and select international destinations — please check checkout for availability in your region.

## Returns & Refunds

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied, here's our policy:

- **Return window:** 30 days from the date of delivery.
- **Condition:** Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached.
- **How to start a return:** Email us at returns@example.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid shipping label within 1–2 business days.
- **Refunds:** Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
- **Exchanges:** We're happy to exchange for a different size or color. Note that sale items are final sale and not eligible for returns or exchanges.

## Washing & Care

To keep your items looking their best, please follow these care instructions:

- **Machine wash:** Cold water only, gentle cycle.
- **Do not bleach:** Avoid any bleach or harsh detergents.
- **Tumble dry:** Low heat only, or lay flat to dry for best results.
- **Ironing:** Iron on low heat if needed; do not iron directly on any printed graphics or embellishments.
- **Dry cleaning:** Not recommended unless the care label specifically states it.

Following these guidelines will help extend the life of your garment and keep colors vibrant.
