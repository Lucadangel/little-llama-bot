# Little Llama — FAQ & Brand Guide

## About Us

Little Llama is an exclusive Danish children's clothing brand based in Aarhus, Denmark. We design super soft and unique knitwear made only in natural materials — 100% baby alpaca or 80% baby alpaca with 20% silk. The collection is inspired by the Nordic minimalist style with unique details, made from the finest baby alpaca yarn of the highest quality for babies and small children aged 0–5 years.

Our vision is to combine the natural yarn from alpacas in the Peruvian highlands with the Danish tradition of design and good craftsmanship. Baby alpaca wool is one of the finest types of yarn worldwide, appealing to those who are both environmentally and fashion conscious. All our products are made with respect for nature and the people involved in the process.

## Washing & Care — Baby Alpaca

Baby alpaca clothing is fantastic and basically does not need to be washed often — it can simply be aired out. Only wash if the item has a stain.

- **Hand wash recommended:** 30°C (hand-warm) water in a dishwashing tub. Keep water temperature constant — baby alpaca cannot tolerate temperature changes.
- **Detergent:** Use detergent specifically for baby alpaca — must be without lanolin and without enzymes. Alternatively, a small teaspoon of organic hair shampoo works. Little Llama also sells organic vegan special soap for baby alpaca.
- **Do not tumble dry.** Lay flat to dry in the correct shape.
- **Baby alpaca is hypoallergenic** — ideal for babies with allergies or eczema, as it contains no lanolin and has a smooth, soft, non-itchy surface.

## Washing & Care — Wool (Merino, Cashmere, Alpaca)

Wool knitwear generally does not need frequent washing — airing out is often enough. Only wash when necessary.

- **Hand wash preferred:** MAX 30°C (finger-warm/hand-warm) water. Keep water temperature constant throughout — wool cannot tolerate temperature changes.
- If machine washing, use a **wool programme at max 30°C** and place the garment in a laundry bag.
- **Do not soak** wool knitwear.
- Use **liquid wool detergent** with a neutral pH. Little Llama also sells organic, vegan, enzyme-free detergents.
- Move the garment carefully in the water — do not rub.
- Rinse in water at the **same temperature** as the wash water.
- **Drying:** Gently remove excess water using a towel or spin lightly in a pillowcase. **Always dry flat** in the correct shape. Never hang a wet wool garment. Avoid direct sunlight.
- **Do not tumble dry** — wool will shrink.
- **Do not press or steam** wool knitwear directly. If needed, use a steam iron with a damp cloth between the iron and the garment.
- **Silk must not be pressed or steamed.**

Note: Untreated wool, angora, mohair, and alpaca must always be hand washed.

Hanging wool clothes outside in cold or freezing weather often cleans them naturally (lanolin self-cleans).

## Size Guide

All sizes are indicated in both centimetres and age. Sizes are based on age, meaning a size fits best for a child at that age:

- **50 cm** — 0 months
- **56/62 cm** — 3–6 months
- **68/74 cm** — 9–12 months
- **80/86 cm** — 12–18 months
- **92 cm** — 2 years
- **98 cm** — 3 years
- **104 cm** — 4 years

For size 4 years, the garment fits throughout the child's 4th year of life (until they turn 5).

Little Llama encourages treating clothes sustainably:

- Recycle responsibly when the garment reaches end of life.
