## Size Guide

All sizes are listed in centimeters and age.  
Example: 56–62 cm / 3–6 months fits best a baby at 6 months, "4 years" size fits until child turns 5.  
| Centimeters | Age           |
|-------------|---------------|
| 50 cm       | 0 months      |
| 56/62 cm    | 3–6 months    |
| 68/74 cm    | 9–12 months   |
| 80/86 cm    | 12–18 months  |
| 92 cm       | 2 years       |
| 98 cm       | 3 years       |
| 104 cm      | 4 years       |
